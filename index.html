Core Features

Experience a meticulously crafted journey where every advancement requires strategic thinking and mastery of nuclear technology.

300+ Deep Quests

Guide you from stone tools to interstellar technologies with logical progression paths and meaningful rewards at every stage.

Full NTM Integration

Nearly all recipes reworked to align with Nuclear Tech philosophy, creating a cohesive and balanced technological journey.

True Challenge

Revised hunger system requiring varied nutrition, smarter mobs, deadlier mechanics, and epic boss battles that test your limits.

Monthly Updates

Continuous development with new content, balance improvements, and bug fixes to keep the experience fresh and engaging.

20+ Languages

Community-driven translations via Crowdin platform ensuring accessibility for players worldwide.

Optimized Performance

Angelica + ArchaicFix integration delivering smooth gameplay even on low-end systems without sacrificing visual quality.

Space Exploration

New dimensions, celestial mechanics, and advanced technologies to conquer the cosmos and unlock humanity's future.
